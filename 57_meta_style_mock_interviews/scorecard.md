# Mock Interview Scorecard

Score each category from 1 to 5.

## 1. Clarity

1: hard to follow  
3: understandable but uneven  
5: clean structure, direct answer, easy to follow

## 2. Technical Correctness

1: major errors  
3: mostly right with gaps  
5: technically solid and defensible

## 3. Assumptions

1: hidden assumptions  
3: some assumptions stated  
5: assumptions explicit and well-managed

## 4. Depth

1: superficial  
3: adequate  
5: can handle follow-ups with substance

## 5. Tradeoff Reasoning

1: ignores trade-offs  
3: mentions some  
5: explains trade-offs clearly and concretely

## 6. Communication Under Pressure

1: loses structure under interruption  
3: somewhat stable  
5: stays composed and adapts cleanly

## Overall Rule

If you score below 4 in more than two categories, repeat the loop until the answer becomes automatic.
